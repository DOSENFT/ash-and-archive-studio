*Bound 2026-07-19 by Marcus's instrument (verbatim ruling on record in ADR-LOG § 2026-07-19). Status: **SEALED — ACTIVE CHARTER.** Supersedes the "interface-first experiment" framing; promotes the design-production layer (formerly "a separate final campaign after behavior is sealed," 00-SPEC-LADDER) to the lead workstream by ADR-EMB-A.*

## §0 — The objective

This is not a redesign of Ash & Archive. It is the **Embodiment Campaign**: prove that a sealed, architecturally complete software system can become a category-defining product through extraordinary execution in industrial design, interaction design, visual systems, motion, information architecture, implementation quality, and craftsmanship — **without altering canonical behavior.**

The software is assumed correct. The architecture is assumed correct. The specifications are assumed correct. The campaign's responsibility is to give them the physical form they deserve.

## §2 — HARD LAW 2 · Zero Behavioral Drift (immutable)

The builder may not invent, merge, simplify, rename, extend, reinterpret, or otherwise modify product behavior. Every improvement must emerge through presentation, interaction, visual communication, motion, discoverability, ergonomics, accessibility, responsiveness, implementation quality, or craftsmanship.

**If a materially better experience appears to require behavioral change, it is documented in `EMBODIMENT-DRIFT-DOCKET.md` — never implemented.** The docket is append-only; each entry names the sealed clause it would touch and the experience it would buy. Docket entries are proposals to the canon holder, nothing more.

## §3 — HARD LAW 3 · SPEC-SH2 is the visual constitution for the entire platform

Lanternlight, Canon Style Block v2, the 1983 practical-effects clause, the material language, environmental authenticity, lighting discipline, and every established visual law of SPEC-SH2 v1.0 (`lanternlight-v1`) govern the software itself — not only the world layer.

Higgsfield does not create a new visual language; it **expresses SH2 across software surfaces**: illustrations, iconography, textures, onboarding, empty states, environmental assets, promotional imagery, and every visual artifact. All generation runs under the standing discipline: shot records before credits burn, HARVEST-INTAKE curation (the canon holder's hand, never self-ratified), the UNCURATED manifest law, and **spend approval before any credit burns** (ASK-FIRST, unchanged).

When Scroll World eventually ships, it must feel like the inevitable spatial extension of this interface, not a separate product.

## §4 — HARD LAW 4 · The platform anchor is explicit

A production-grade, **desktop-first Tauri application with React as the presentation layer**, fully aligned with the existing architecture (the Rust host, the asset protocol, the binding seam of SPEC-001 §1.1). Never drift toward generic SaaS assumptions, browser-first compromises, or placeholder implementation patterns.

## §5 — The measure of success

The experiment is **not** whether we can build a beautiful interface. It is whether industrial-grade execution alone can elevate an already-correct product into something that permanently raises expectations for creative software.

The benchmark is not D&D Beyond, World Anvil, Obsidian, Notion, Linear, Figma, Arc, or Unreal Engine. The benchmark is whether experienced designers, principal engineers, product leaders, technical artists, and software architects encounter Ash & Archive and immediately recognize a genuine leap in software craftsmanship — quality that feels **authored rather than assembled, inevitable rather than designed, timeless rather than fashionable.**

This sits atop the standing bar (SHIP-LEDGER): software that makes a professional's ten-hour day calmer and faster; reviewers say "of course"; superior by discipline, never spectacle.

## §6 — Working doctrine

Approach every decision with first-principles reasoning. Challenge assumptions before implementing them. Search for stronger architectures before refining weaker ones. Continuously self-audit for inconsistencies, hidden constraints, implementation debt, interaction friction, aesthetic compromise, accessibility regressions, performance bottlenecks, scalability concerns, and deviations from canon. **Verify every deliverable against the sealed specifications before considering it complete.** Produce production-ready systems, not demonstrations.

Standing governance is unchanged and applies in full: branch discipline (spec branches; main by seal-merge) · every non-dictated decision an ADR or a recorded build-report decision, never silent · fresh-context adversarial verification for spec-grade artifacts · the ship ledger tracks blockers append-only.

No verification, seal, or spend proceeds while parked. It reopens, by Marcus's instrument only, as the spatial extension of a shipped embodied interface — a force multiplier layered onto a mature platform, not the platform's definition.
